Controls

Click a region to focus it. Double click a directory to descend. Right click or Backspace to go to parent. Mouse wheel or pinch-equivalent trackpad gesture to zoom. Drag the field to pan. Press / to focus search. Press L to toggle labels. Press G to toggle glow. JSON format: { "name": "root", "type": "dir", "children": [ {"name": "notes", "type": "dir", "children": [...]}, {"name": "paper.tex", "type": "file", "size": 1200} ] }
